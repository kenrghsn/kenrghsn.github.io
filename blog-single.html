Student Lighthouse Team

A Student Lighthouse Team is a student-led group in schools that promotes leadership, implements the 7 Habits of Highly Effective People, and fosters a positive school culture by acting as role models and providing student voice for school improvement initiatives. Members, selected through applications and interviews, represent their peers, run school events like Leadership Day, and help build relationships and character within the school community.

We are the first generation of SLHT in this school. In this organization, I work in the academic division. Academic achievement is one of the most important factors used to determine a school's success. When I was part of the academic division, I was responsible for monitoring each class' academic scoreboard to see if it was aligned with the Widely Important Goals (WIGs).

This is a photo from SLHT High School's first Leadership Day. I was the tour guide that day, showing the guests around the decorated classrooms. Upon arrival, the classroom staff explained the various physical environments and their functions.
